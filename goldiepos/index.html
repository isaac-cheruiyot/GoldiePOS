Welcome to Goldie POS

14 Years Experience

Goldie POS, based in Kenya, is dedicated to providing innovative Point of Sale solutions that cater to the unique needs of various industries. Our goal is to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and customer satisfaction through advanced technology and exceptional service. With a commitment to quality and reliability, Goldie POS is the trusted partner for businesses looking to streamline their operations and boost profitability.

Learn More

Point of Sale Features

  • Quick Sale Mode/Walk-in and existing customer sales.
  • Accepts Barcode scan mode and Product search mode.
  • Ability to put transactions on hold & proceed with other sales then Resume pending sales/bill.
  • Accepts multiple modes of payment method (MPESA, PDQ/Card, and CASH & Credit Sales).
  • Accepts multiple modes of payment per Single transaction e.g. (200sh cash and 300sh MPESA, 500sh Total).
  • Flexible Retail, Wholesale, and custom prices editing modes.
  • Ability to sell items in decimal quantities e.g. (0.1 quantity of an item) and auto deduct quantities.
  • Expense management.
  • Ability to Cancel Sales Order and its report.
  • Low stock below reorder levels notifications and reports.
  • Allow Receipt reprinting.
  • Fiscal and Non-Fiscal Receipts generation.
  • Customer details and balances.
  • Customer invoice Management.
  • Has secure customer received payment security feature that controls/shows who received Payment, date and time and prints receipt.
  • Customer Sales Orders and Statements.
  • Staff item sales summary report.
  • Allow sales Per Till.
  • Customer Quotation generation.
  • Accepts Vatable and Non-Vatable Tax per item.
  • Has touch enabled Interface.
  • System backup and Security Settings.

Stock Inventory / Control and Purchase Management

  • Supplier Details.
  • Cash Purchases Orders.
  • Credit Purchase Orders.
  • Purchase Returns.
  • Product/Item detailed list with description, categorization, Unit, and Adding.
  • Ability to Apply VAT inclusive or exclusive to specific product.
  • Ability to do Stock Taking which captures who received, when, supplier and product received.
  • Ability to do Stocking Adjustments with reason for adjustment features Contained.
  • Ability to receive stock with expiry dates for items like medicine and perishable goods.
  • Ability to track stock movement and its report.
  • Flexible Price Change per item management.
  • System has Stock Value for the shop.
  • Has Stock at hand and Valuation report.
  • Re-order Level Processing.

Reporting

  • Sales by item detailed and summary report.
  • Sales by staff detailed and summary report.
  • Sales by day, weekly, monthly and yearly Summary Report.
  • Sales to specific Customer summary report.
  • Mode of payment sales summary report.
  • Cancelled sales by staff summary report.
  • Top selling item sorted by Quantity and amount report.
  • Sales by Till report.
